About Space Exploration

Space exploration involves the investigation of outer space through the use of astronomy and space technologies. It has led to significant discoveries about our universe, including the moon landings, Mars rovers, and the search for extraterrestrial life.

Current Space Missions

  • NASA's Perseverance Rover - Exploring Mars and searching for signs of ancient life.
  • SpaceX's Starship - Preparing for human missions to Mars.
  • James Webb Space Telescope - Observing distant galaxies to better understand the universe.
